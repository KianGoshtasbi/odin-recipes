Hotdog Recipe

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Hot dogs
  • Mustard

Cooking Steps

  1. Set hot dogs on a flaming hot grill (Be sure to move hot dog around as it cooks)
  2. Take hot dog off after 4-6 minutes of cooking
  3. Place hot dog in the hot dog buns
  4. Put mustard on hot dog and enjoy!
