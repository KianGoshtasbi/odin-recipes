Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced sausage
  • Sliced onions
  • Marinara sauce
  • Any other toppings you would like

Cooking steps

  1. Evenly spread marinara sauce along the pizza dough
  2. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on the marinara sauce
  3. Evenly spread your remaining toppings along the mozzarella cheese
  4. Place in an oven at 400 degrees fahrenheit for approximately 10-15 minutes
    (Take out once crust looks fully formed)
